This animation allows for testing the following features:

  • Player Controls
  • Sync between Audio and animation
  • Sync between symbols and the main timeline
  • Symbol triggering at various times

Please note, the yellow time is the player's time, based on the audio track. The big number in the middle is the animation's primary timeline (the stage). The circle is a symbol that is playing back for the full length of the timeline, but it has its own timeline that we need to make sure stays in sync. The numbers that float by are individual symbols that are triggered at various times.
